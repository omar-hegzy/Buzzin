    Buzzin FZCO is a software development company based in Dubai – UAE.

    We are focused on developing smart business applications that make daily life easier. Rapid enhancements in technology have created a new normal, a new paradigm to which we have all had to adapt and keep up with – especially those in business who want to remain in business. As the world is moving towards a “low touch economy” post Covid-19 pandemic, contactless based solutions will thrive. Our solutions are designed to ensure a contactless experience thereby guaranteeing convenience and safety. Our subscription based model makes our solutions affordable to all sizes and types of business, as no capital investment is needed. At Buzzin, we are determined to add convenience to your everyday life.

    Enabling smart sustainable communities and cities.
