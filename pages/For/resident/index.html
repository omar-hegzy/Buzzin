    • Buzzin for Residents

    Experience seamless, contactless access with the Buzzin app, ensuring convenience and security for residents and visitors alike.

    With the Buzzin app, UAE residents can enjoy a contactless access solution through their virtual NFC card. Our app also enables them to grant virtual access passes to friends, visitors, or guests, ensuring smooth entry without manual registration at reception. Instant notifications are received by residents whenever someone arrives at the reception, offering convenience for both hosts and visitors. This feature also enhances safety by allowing residents to approve or pre-authorize entry only through the Buzzin app.
