    Buzzin for
    office
    Buzzin gives you accurate visibility of the traffic flow in your commercial workspace.
    Buzzin for Office
    Our solution will allow you to have detailed information on who is coming into your commercial office space. You can even see the traffic flow per office or per department. Buzzin App is perfect for commercial centers with leased offices, corporate headquarters and branches. Visitor traffic is managed in a more secure and more convenient way. This way you can ensure a seamless and positive customer experience.
    Buzzin for
    Schools
    Buzzin makes for more secure school premises and therefore more reassured parents with our contactless entry.
    Buzzin for School
    Students’ security and safety are a top priority for both parents and schools. Buzzin enhances schools’ security by managing visitors’ entry in a more efficient way. Our visitor management system offers contactless entry, which means less Covid risk. Schools can record the proper identification of visitors without the hassle of manual registration. Our contactless solution eliminates queueing at reception and ensures a smooth flow of visitors. Buzzin can also be customized to any particular school’s individual requirements.
    Buzzin for
    Residents & Appartments
    Convenient, safe, and easy to use, the Buzzin app is the smart solution that smart property owners offer to residents.
    Buzzin for Residents & Appartments
    Via the Buzzin app residents will benefit from a seamless and contactless access solution that offers an impressive welcome to their visitors. All residents and tenants need is a smartphone and the Buzzin app. Residents can then grant friends/visitors/guests a virtual access pass that allows them to smoothly access the premises without the hassle of manual registration at reception.
    Buzzin for
    Property Owners
    Buzzin is a contactless solution that makes for more secure residential towers and communities.
    Buzzin for Property Owners
    Buzzin allows you to shift your visitor’s registration process from an existing manual system that may capture inaccurate or incomplete data (and be open to data breaches), to something more sophisticated, yet simple and convenient. All you need is to pop the tablet we supply onto your reception desk. No wiring, no onsite servers, and no financial outlay. And no technical expertise is needed to set it up. The Buzzin dashboard for property owners offers impressive data analytics tools that provide you with full control of accurate data that is stored securely in the cloud.
