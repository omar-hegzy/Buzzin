    Buzzin For Delivery
    Buzzin means busy delivery drivers no longer have to waste time hanging around at reception desks to sign in.     office
    Buzzin gives you accurate visibility of the traffic flow in your commercial workspace.
    Buzzin for Office
    Our solution will allow you to have detailed information on who is coming into your commercial office space. You can even see the traffic flow per office or per department. Buzzin App is perfect for commercial centers with leased offices, corporate headquarters and branches. Visitor traffic is managed in a more secure and more convenient way. This way you can ensure a seamless and positive customer experience. We will create a username for you, where you will have access to a dashboard that shows all the information you need. As for the hosts, they will receive a notification message once their visitor or guest arrives at the premises. The hosts are given the option of either accepting or reporting their visitor. Access can also be restricted to pre-authorized visitors. With Buzzin, the security will no longer need to contact the host once the visitor or guest arrives at the premises.
    Buzzin for
    Schools
    Buzzin makes for more secure school premises and therefore more reassured parents with our contactless entry.
    Buzzin for School
    Students’ security and safety are a top priority for both parents and schools. Buzzin enhances schools’ security by managing visitors’ entry in a more efficient way. Our visitor management system offers contactless entry, which means less Covid risk. Schools can record the proper identification of visitors without the hassle of manual registration. Our contactless solution eliminates queueing at reception and ensures a smooth flow of visitors. Buzzin can also be customized to any particular school’s individual requirements.
    Buzzin for
    Residents/Appartments
    Convenient, safe, and easy to use, the Buzzin app is the smart solution that smart property owners offer to residents.
    Buzzin for Residents/Appartments
    Via the Buzzin app residents will benefit from a seamless and contactless access solution that offers an impressive welcome to their visitors. All residents and tenants need is a smartphone and the Buzzin app. Residents can then grant friends/visitors/guests a virtual access pass that allows them to smoothly access the premises without the hassle of manual registration at reception. Residents will receive instant notifications whenever someone buzzes in through the reception/gate to visit. This is incredibly convenient for both host and visitor, and means you can keep yourself safe by only letting in those you approve or pre-authorise via the Buzzin app.
    Buzzin for
    Property Owners
    Buzzin is a contactless solution that makes for more secure residential towers and communities.
    Buzzin for Property Owners
    Buzzin allows you to shift your visitor’s registration process from an existing manual system that may capture inaccurate or incomplete data (and be open to data breaches), to something more sophisticated, yet simple and convenient. All you need is to pop the tablet we supply onto your reception desk. No wiring, no onsite servers, and no financial outlay. And no technical expertise is needed to set it up. The Buzzin dashboard for property owners offers impressive data analytics tools that provide you with full control of accurate data that is stored securely in the cloud. Buzzin is not simply an app, it’s a GDPR compliant solution offering guaranteed data security. You will have real-time accurate and up-to-date information on visitors to your building, therefore offering greater security and peace of mind. The Buzzin app allows for contactless entry, meaning your building or community will be Covid-19 safe for residents, visitors and staff. By offering Buzzin in your building your reception staff/building managers can focus on other areas and offer greater service and added value to residents. Buzzin will make you stand out from the crowd as an early adopter of new tech offering a better standard of professional and convenient service to your residents with a solution that complies with the strictest privacy policies. Communities: No need to handwrite plate numbers which takes time and creates traffic jams at the entrance of your community. Have it all captured via our ANPL cameras, making for a much smoother visitor experience? Make sure visitors are actually going to the right villa, townhouse, or apartment by obtaining confirmation from the host in advance.

    How it works

    All you have to do is take out a subscription for our services. A sales representative will visit the manager of your premises beforehand to assess your requirements. Since the solution is cloud based, we will not require access to your datacenter and there, no server or hardware will be deployed on-site apart from a simple android tablet placed on the stand provided by Buzzin. After subscribing, users within your premises can then download the app and sign up for a swift accesss.
